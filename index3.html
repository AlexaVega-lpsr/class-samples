Raichu

Raichu is a orange kangaroo mouse, bipedal like pokemon

Raichu is the evolved form of Pikachu, it is an electric type with the power of releasing 10,000 volts of electricity at will

It's main color is a type of dark orange with brown stripes on it's back, a tail with a lightning bolt at the tip, and big crescent ears, it has yellow cheeks, and a white tummy.
